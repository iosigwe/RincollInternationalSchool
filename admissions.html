Admissions

We are delighted that you are considering RINCOLL International School for your child. Our admissions process is designed to be simple, transparent, and supportive.

Step 1: Make an Enquiry

Parents and guardians are encouraged to contact the school to obtain information about available classes and entry requirements.

Step 2: Complete Application

Complete the admission application form and submit all required documents for assessment.

Step 3: Assessment & Interview

Prospective students may be invited for an assessment or interview depending on the class level.

Step 4: Admission Offer

Successful applicants will receive an admission offer and further enrollment details.
